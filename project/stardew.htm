Review: Stardew Valley

Overview: 9.0/10. Stardew Valley is a fun, wholesome game that is great for casual players and gamers alike. The recent addition of multiplayer capabilities complements the overall mood of the game as it allows players to work together in a calm, cooperative way. My only complaint is that after the main storyline is over, there is very little left to do in the game, and I hope that there will be new features to keep the game intersting soon. The game is definitely priced well at $15 on Steam, and only $8 on iOS. I would recommend.
